## Section 1: What FastMCP does well

FastMCP is one of the most popular open-source frameworks for building MCP servers.
If you're a Python developer who wants to prototype an MCP server quickly, it's
an excellent starting point. Its decorator-based API is clean, the community is
active, and it's free to use.

We built Vendia because we kept seeing the same pattern: teams would prototype
with FastMCP, then spend months building the production infrastructure around it.
Auth. Rate limiting. Logging. Uptime. Monitoring. None of that is in FastMCP —
and all of it is table stakes for production.

---

## Section 2: The hidden cost of self-hosting

When you run FastMCP in production, you own:

- **Auth and access control** — SSO integration, per-user and per-tool permissions
- **Rate limiting** — preventing abuse and runaway agent costs
- **Audit logging** — who called what tool, when, with what arguments
- **Uptime** — on-call rotation, incident response, SLA accountability
- **Security** — vulnerability patching, network hardening, secret management

That's not a weekend project. For most teams it's 2–4 months of engineering time
and ongoing maintenance — for infrastructure that isn't your product.

## Section 3: What Vendia gives you instead

Vendia is a managed MCP Gateway. You connect your data sources — APIs, databases,
S3 buckets — and Vendia exposes them as MCP tools. Auth, rate limiting, logging,
and uptime are handled. You configure; we operate.

- **Up in hours, not months** — connect your first data source and get a
  production-ready MCP server the same day
- **Built-in governance** — every tool call logged, access controlled per source,
  PII masking available
- **Multi-party data sharing** — Vendia's platform is purpose-built for sharing
  data across organizational boundaries, not just within a single app

## Section 5: When FastMCP is the right choice

FastMCP is the right tool if you're:
- An individual developer building a personal or internal prototype
- A team that wants full control over the implementation and has the engineering
  capacity to own production operations
- Evaluating MCP before committing to managed infrastructure

If that's you, FastMCP is great. Come back when you're ready to move to production.

## Section 6: When Vendia is the right choice

Vendia is the right tool if you're:
- A team that needs MCP servers running reliably in production

- Done spending engineering cycles on infrastructure that isn't your product
